## Hook

Most people overestimate what they can do in a day — and drastically underestimate what they can do in a year.

## Body

Consistency beats intensity every single time.

We often look for the big breakthrough moment: the perfect opportunity, the viral post, the lucky break.

But the people who truly grow in their careers are not the ones who sprint — they are the ones who show up, day after day, even when it is hard.

Here is what consistent effort looks like in practice:

- Writing one post a week instead of waiting for the "perfect" idea
- Learning one new concept every day instead of binge-studying once a month
- Having one meaningful conversation every week instead of waiting for networking events

Small actions, repeated over time, compound into extraordinary results.

You do not need more motivation. You need a system that makes showing up the default.
